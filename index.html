The Front-End Committee at IEEE

The Front-End Committee at IEEE focuses on teaching and developing skills in web design and front-end development.Our goal is to help students understand the fundamentals of web technologies — from HTML,CSS, and JavaScript to building real projects hands-on. We organize weekly learning sessions covering different web concepts and encourage members to practice and grow through real-world implementation.
